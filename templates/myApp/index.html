About Me

Hello Friend! Thanks for viewing my portfolio - it's no coincidence you're here. You're curious about me and what I've been up to, read on!

I'm a 30 year old self taught software developer and entreprenuer. I'm passionate about thoughtfully using technology to solve real problems that we all run into as humans. From my diverse background I developed a unique ear tuned for the little voice that says "wouldn't it be great if..." and I'm compelled to quickly go test those solutions. For most of my adult life I worked outside of the technology industry - oftentimes using software to supercharge my capabilities as a worker. After being unable to bring my ideas to life for so long, I jumped head first into learning software development! Now, I'm able to apply those many years of experience spanning from EMS/Fire, Military, and Business to my daily work.

Today you can find me working on new products or busy developing software for businesses and artists in Miami, FL and abroad. Whether I'm designing an Artist's marketing website or creating new revenue streams for a CEO, I take joy in finding the "secrets" that one only comes across when doing the hard thigns. By getting my hands dirty and doing the things others won't I synthesize truth and value. The engines of all good business. I am at my best when my routine is "squared away" and includes a lot of excersize, journaling, and time blocked out to strategicly hit goals. Like an engine I like to fire on all cyclendars - and I am always tweaking and testing to find better results!
